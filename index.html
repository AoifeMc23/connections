Artist Statement

Hello! My name is Aoife McNamara and I am a graduate of Creative Digital Media BA hons, Munster Technological University Cork. I am a professional artist, in the early stages of their career, whose work focuses on connecting people with tehcnology in shared spaces.

"Connect together, not alone"

The methodolgies behind my work involves thorough experiments where I explore the limits of my skills with different technologies and how best they can create an experience for the audience.

I focus on exploring and testing technolgies that can bring the audience together in shared spaces.

My approach and methodolgu of bringing people together using technology could be used in exchange for a specific theme or topics in different spaces, for example in a museum space with a particular topic.
